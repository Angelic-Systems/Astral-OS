# Contributing to Astral OS

Thank you for considering contributing to **Astral OS**!  
This is a small, personal, and strictly **TUI-first** Void Linux-based distro. 
The goal is to keep it lightweight, clean, and focused on a great terminal experience (Zellij + zjstatus, labwc, kitty, etc.).

**Reproducible** Include steps to reproduce the problem.
**Specific** Include as much detail as possible: which version, what environment, etc.
**Unique** Do not duplicate existing opened issues.

## Code of Conduct

By participating in this project, you agree to follow our (CODE_OF_CONDUCT.md). Be kind, respectful, and constructive.

## How Can I Contribute?

There are many ways to help, even small ones:

- **Reporting bugs** or suggesting improvements
- **Improving documentation** (README, this file, setup guides)
- **Creating or refining configs** (zjstatus, River/labwc, Wofi/Fuzzel, Plymouth theme, dotfiles)
- **Writing useful scripts** (first-boot setup, better status bar commands, etc.)
- **Submitting TUI-related tools or improvements** (launcher, custom zjstatus modules, etc.)
- **Testing** on different hardware 
- **Spreading the word** (blog posts, sharing your setup)
